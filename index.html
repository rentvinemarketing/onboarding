how it works

From signed to live in about six weeks.

A clear path with no guesswork. Here's exactly what happens, who's driving it, and what you can expect at each step.

1
you + onboarding specialist

Kickoff & setup

Your onboarding specialist reaches out right away. You'll share company info, billing, door count and your chart of accounts, and get a full walkthrough of what's ahead in OnRamp.

2
2–4 orientation calls

Configure your account

We tailor Rentvine to how you actually operate: account and bank info, management and late fee structure, accounting settings, owner and resident portals, and maintenance setup.

3
rentvine migration team

Migrate your data

You pick a cut-off date to stop entering transactions in your old software. We migrate your active owners, properties, residents, leases and vendors into Rentvine.

4
you + financial analyst

Financial balance forward

Your analyst helps you complete a Balance Forward Workbook: unpaid bills, resident charges, prepayments, owner balances, security deposits, and owner and vendor 1099s. We validate it, then load it in.

5
rentvine, for you

Reconcile & go live

We complete a full reconciliation of your bank accounts so your brought-forward balances match to the dollar. Forte merchant setup is finished, and electronic payments are switched on.

6
you + account manager

Graduate & grow

You meet your dedicated account manager and move into ongoing support. Onboarding is done, but training and help never stop.

  • Accounting: money in, money out, bills and management fees
  • Maintenance, work orders and diagnostics
  • RentSign, reporting, renewals and move-outs
  • Contacts, portals, dashboards and global settings
